Mini PD Sink

A board to negotiate USB Power Delivery contracts and output them on solder points. Small enough to hide in a cable (11x17mm).
This is designed to allow simple projects to be powered from USB PD bricks and batteries at up to 100W (20V@5A) while being discretely placed in a cable.
